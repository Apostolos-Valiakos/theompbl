About Us

A modern company built for digital commerce.

ATRIA COMMERCE is a single-member private company based in Larisa, Greece. The company operates in the broader field of ecommerce, internet services, and digital advertising.

Its business activity includes online retail-related services, website and internet-based services, digital promotion, and related commercial activity carried out through modern online channels.
